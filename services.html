Cogent Coaching Services

Together we extend our focus to moving into action. We get to the "how" and "when" you practically implement and grow with your life changes. Cogent Coaching offers services for:

Young Professionals seeking career development:

    • Career Planning
      Job Search
      Planning Professional Development
      Developing an exit strategy or transition plan
      Succeeding as a new Executive Director
      Interview Preperation

    • Private Sector Executives:

    • Transitioning from manager to leader
      Enhancing leadership skills
      Communicating vision and engaging others
      Strengthening and managing board relationships
      Developing staff leaders
      Managing change
      Finding balance in work and life

    • Private Sector Managers:

    • Enhancing supervisory skills
      Managing time effectively
      Organizing and planning projects, prioritizing work
      Delegating
      Managing up
      Managing stress

    • Expatriates:

    • Managing the transition to a new culture
      Introduction to the values, attitudes and behaviors in the new country
      How to ask for things that you need, when you need them
      How to make friends and develop good relationships with colleagues
      Workplace traditions and principles
      Understanding culture shock and how to deal with unease effectively
      Special training for children and spouses

    • Coaching Sessions:

    • 45 or 60 minute Private One on One Sessions
      15 minute Check-In Sessions
