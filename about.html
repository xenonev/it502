Introducing the Coach

With over 20 years of leadership, management and consulting experience in the corporate world, I have helped clients enhance all aspects of their work and personal lives. Based on the strong belief that awareness and insight are key to identifying solutions, I have provided guidance and motivation to clients, employees, partners, and colleagues. I enhanced their impact, effectiveness and personal sense of achievement through coaching.My open, judgment-free, and supportive approach sets the stage for dialogue and a creative space to design and successfully achieve goals and visions in all areas of life. My services particularly focused on helping people navigate life transitions and personal challenges related to the following:

  • Career growth
  • Leadership skills
  • Cultural transitions
  • Childrearing concerns
  • Relationships
  • Interpersonal conflict
  • Divorce

Bio

I received my Professional Coach Certification (CPC) through the Institute for Professional Excellence in Coaching (iPEC). She is also certified as an Energy Leadership Index™ (ELI) Master Practitioner. ELI is an attitudinal assessment that identifies current leadership perspectives and actions, analyzes their effectiveness, and provides tools and strategies to overcome obstacles to success. I also received certifications in the field of mediation and conflict resolution from Harvard Law School. A native of Vienna, Austria, I earned a degree in Econometrics from Pantheon Sorbonne University in Paris and also holds a degree in Tourism and Hospitality Management from Modul University in Vienna. Before relocating to the U.S. where I currently am a Principal and Managing Director at Dalbar, Inc., I worked in Mexico City as International Investment Associate at the Deutsch Südamerikanische Bank, a subsidiary of Dresdner Bank, Germany, as well as freelance correspondent for the publication “Austria Business & Economy” in Paris, France.
